About Us:

Great Prices For
Quality Cars

Welcome to Duncan Auto Brokerz.We are a family-owned business consisting of Master Automotive Technicians with over 30 years of sales and automotive experience. Being in this line of work for so long, we have cultivated the highest set of standards for each car that we sell.
Our quality control process begins by selecting only the most dependable used cars to purchase. From there, our technicians inspect each vehicle thoroughly from bumper to bumper, inside and out. We make sure all the necessary repairs are done so that you are buying the safest, most reliable car you can get. If a car does not meet our rigorous list of requirements, it will not be sold by us.
When you come to us, you don't deal with the run of the mill salesman trying to empty your pockets. Our team will take the time to focus on you and provide a one-on-one specialized experience. Because we purchase, inspect, and repair each car, we can answer all the questions that you have. We will work with you to get the right price and walk you through the car buying process just like you are family. Customer service and client satisfaction are always our number one priority.
